# DogEar Privacy Policy DogEar respects your privacy. ## Data We Collect - Account information (username, email) - Videos you choose to save via supported platforms - Push notification tokens (used only to deliver notifications) ## How We Use Data - To provide core app functionality - To sync saved videos - To send optional notifications ## What We Do NOT Do - We do not sell your data - We do not show a public feed - We do not track you across other apps ## Third-Party Services DogEar uses: - OneSignal (notifications) - RevenueCat (subscriptions) - Google AdMob (ads) These services process data according to their own privacy policies. ## Contact For questions: support@dogear.app
